Who Am I?

I am currently a 3D Artist at Funomena.

Storytelling is what drove me to pursue a career in art. As a kid I loved to read, but in later I fell in love with how film and games tell stories create experiences in visual and interactive ways.

I also have a passion for science and education. 3D art appeals to me because it requires an interest in learning about a variety of subjects, a love of art, and the ability to use and understand technology. Using technology to create art is what I do..
